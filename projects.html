As IT and Engineering students of ADA University, we "Shamsi Nagiev", "Pasha Zulfugarli", "Riad Allahverdiyev",and "Rahman Karimov" gathered together and decided to host the Hour Of Code event in one of the Secondary Schools in Baku. We worked as a team to deliver a brief overview of the ADA university to a class of sixth graders. We also gave brief information regarding programming languages and demonstrated how to use the drag-and-drop learning tool in Code.org to help students comprehend the reasoning behind programming languages. We concluded our lesson by awarding certificates to those who actively participated in the course, which served to stimulate the pupils.
